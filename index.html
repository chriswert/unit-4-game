Star Trek Collector

Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.


There are four Star Trek Captains below. By clicking on a Captain you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.


You win the game by matching your total score to random numbers. You loose the game if your total score goes about the random number.


The value of each Captain is hidden from you untill you click on it.


Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each Captain.
